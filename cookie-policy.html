Privacy Policy

At BITBANCE.AI, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data while you use our AI-powered crypto trading platform. Let’s keep it simple and transparent!

1. What Information Do We Collect?

- Personal Information: Name, email address, and contact details when you sign up.
- Account Information: Username, password, and preferences.
- Trading Data: Information related to your trades, such as transaction history and performance metrics.
- Technical Data: IP address, device information, and browser type for security and analytics.

2. How Do We Use Your Information?

- Provide Services: Enable automated trading and ensure the platform functions smoothly.
- Improve Performance: Analyze data to enhance our AI algorithms and accuracy.
- Ensure Security: Protect your account and prevent fraud.
- Communicate: Send updates, notifications, and support responses.

3. How Do We Protect Your Data?

Your security is our top priority. We use advanced encryption, secure protocols, and regular audits to protect your data. Additionally, we recommend enabling two-factor authentication (2FA) for added account security.

4. Do We Share Your Information?

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share data with:
- Trusted Partners: Service providers who help us operate the platform (e.g., cloud hosting, payment processors).
- Legal Authorities: If required by law or to protect our rights and users.

5. Your Rights and Choices

- Access, update, or delete your personal information.
- Opt-out of marketing communications.
- Disable your account at any time.
To exercise these rights, contact us at support@bitbance.ai.

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to improve your experience, analyze usage, and personalize content. You can manage cookie preferences in your browser settings.

7. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to provide our services, comply with legal obligations, or resolve disputes. Rest assured, we won’t keep your data longer than needed.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We’ll notify you of any significant updates, and continued use of BITBANCE.AI means you accept the revised policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about your privacy, please reach out to our support team at support@bitbance.ai. We’re here to help!

Final Note

BITBANCE.AI is committed to keeping your data safe and secure while providing you with the best automated trading experience. Thank you for trusting us with your crypto trading journey!
